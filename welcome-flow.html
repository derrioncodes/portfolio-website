Welcome Flow

Overview

In this project we are redesigning YoungLA's welcome flow. They are very graphic heavy in their emails but since they are growing rapidly their emails need to be more accessible to a wide audience but still look great.

Their target audience our people who enjoy working out and want to look good in and out the gym. They are the ages of 16 - 30. The brand voice will be relatable to the younger generation as if we are a friend who wants the best for them.

Steps

Once I knew the target audience and brand voice I created a simple outline of how I wanted the flow to go. Then I created wireframes in google docs for the copy. Once the copy was done I used reallygoodemails.com to get ideas of how the information needed to be displayed based on the purpose of each email. Then I used Figma to design the emails.

Pop Up Form

We are assuming for this project they already have ads and/or social media post that direct potential customers to their website. In that case we have created a pop up form to capture emails to kickstart our Welcome Flow emails.

Desktop

Mobile

Elements of our pop up form

1. Targeting & Time delay

For targeting we want to make sure existing subscribers don't see this form and people who are about to purchase a product don't see this form because we don't want interrupt their experience and let them checkout.

For this pop up form we want to make sure our pop up shows after a 5 - 7 second delay or when they scroll 50% of the page. This gives the visitor a chance to look around and not be annoyed as soon as they get on the site. We also want to make sure visitors that have seen the form don't see it again for another 15 days.

2. Copy

We are going to keep this simple by just showing the offer. With our target audience being younger we want to get straight to the point.

3. Testing

There are several ways I would test this pop-up as we gather data. I would focus on testing one element at a time:

  • Offering a different incentive: Experimenting with a new offer. Is 15% off compelling enough for our visitors, or could another incentive perform better?
  • Testing the layout: Placing the copy over a background image instead of keeping the image to the right. Would this be more aesthetically pleasing to our visitors?
  • Adding body copy: Including additional text below the headline. Does our audience need more information on why they should sign up?

Email 1

Welcome

Purpose

Deliver a strong intro to the brand in the briefest way possible while giving them what they want, the discount.

Content

  • Deliver incentive
  • Introduce brand unique selling propositions
  • Social proof
  • Show bestsellers

Goal

The key here is to keep things brief. People get to this point because of they want the discount we offered in our pop up form. The goal is for them to click the button, learn a little bit about the brand, and finish shopping.

Email 2

Our Story

Purpose

Share our brand background to bring the subscriber closer to the brand and bought into the mission.

Content

  • Founding story
  • Mission statement
  • Show a face if possible (put a face to the name —> personal feel)
  • Reminder of discount

Goal

We want to build trust in this email. Showing that we care and have a real mission. The Goal is to relate to our subscriber with our mission and why we do what we do and then remind them of there discount. Making them feel more connected with us and priming them for future purchases.

Email 3

Best Sellers

Purpose

Show off our bestseller(s) and tell the subscriber why we stand out from our competitors.

Content

  • Highlight bestseller(s)
  • Explain why it's so good
  • Remind of discount

Goal

Go in depth of what makes our products so good. This is the perfect time to explain any features or benefits that separate our products from the our competition. We will also remind them of their discount then send them to our best sellers page.

Email 4

Hurry!

Purpose

We are going to introduce urgency and remind the subscriber of their discount to get them to buy on the spot. And to get them over to edge we'll add testimonials to show others love our products.

Content

  • Add urgency to offer
  • Risk reversals
  • Give testimonials for trust

Goal

At this point the subscriber knows about our brand and believes we have great products. Now it's time to add urgency by letting them know this discount will expire. We will show 5 star reviews of products our customers love to help them buy.

Email 5

FAQs

Purpose

Answer frequently asked question to eliminate any concerns they might have.

Content

  • Let them know we are aware they may have more questions
  • Add our top FAQS

Goal

If our subscriber hasn't purchased at this point they most likely have objections we haven't handled. We will have out top FAQ's in this email to answer any questions they may have. Then remind them of the discount so they can happily shop.

Email 6

Testimonials

Purpose

Further prove our brand is reputable and that our products create happy customers.

Content

  • Brand testimonials
  • Media features
  • Any content that proves our brand is legit.

Goal

Our subscriber needs to know our products are great and they have nothing to worry about when it's time for them to purchase. We will show 5 star reviews from our customers and add risk reversals like “free shipping” and “secure payments” to ensure they feel comfortable shopping with us.

Email 7

Last Chance

Purpose

Give the customer a last chance to use their discount to try to get them over the edge.

Content

  • Time or urgency based graphic
  • Your discount is expiring
  • Show products the may love
  • Give them a sneak peak into what's coming next to add excitement

Goal

Our subscribers have all they need to to make a purchase. Maybe they are waiting for the right time. In this case we will add extreme urgency so they can use their discount.

Email 8

Message from Founder

Purpose

We want to give a personal reminder and last chance message that really let's the customer know we care about them.

Content

  • Text based email
  • Remind of discount code
  • Offer resources

Goal

This email will be a texted based email to add a more personal touch from the founder. If they still haven't purchased, maybe they have more questions so we want to give them an email and phone number where they can reach out to us.

If they haven't opened any of these emails or clicked on any of our buttons or links we will take them off our list to keep our data clean.
