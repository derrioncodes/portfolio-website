Abandon Cart Flow

Overview

In this project we are creating an abandon cart flow for Dr. Squatch. The purpose of this abandoned cart email flow is to re-engage potential customers who have added items to their shopping cart but left the website without completing their purchase.

There are 3 areas we want to address when building out our flow.

1. Length of the flow - we don't want the flow to be to short and miss key touch points in our potential customer's buying decisions. We need to give them multiple reminders, overcome objections, and give them enough time to make a decision. This will allow us to stand out from our competitors.

2. Effective copy - our copy needs to be quick and to the point. It also needs to be specific to their journey and the products they are interested in. It should address common objections, such as concerns about shipping, product usage, or value. Including answers to FAQs helps build trust and confidence, making it easier for the customer to complete their purchase.

3. Effective design - similar to the copy it should be quick and to the point. Clear headline addressing their cart, a button they can see as soon as they open the email that let's them finish shopping, and showing the item(s) they left behind with relevant information.

This automated email sequence aims to:

  • Recover Lost Revenue
  • Remind Customers
  • Address Hesitations
  • Create Urgency
  • Personalize their Experience
  • Foster Brand Loyalty

Email 1

Quick Reminder

Purpose

Remind the customer of what they left behind.

Content

  • Brief statement addressing their product(s)
  • Dynamic product section
  • Social proof/risk reversal

Goal

Show the customer what they left behind and give them a chance to finish their order.

Email 2

This email will convert anyone else who is ready to buy from a simple reminder. We will also add in risk reversals so they are comfortable finishing their purchase.

Email 5A

Last Chance

Purpose

Use a personal founder touch to get a last ditch effort at conversion. We also want to offer support for if the customer has questions.

Content

  • Text based email from the founder
  • Give a last chance to order
  • Offer support for any questions

Goal

This email serves as the final opportunity for past buyers to complete their purchase. If the customer is a repeat buyer who didn't complete their cart this time, we'll let them go after this. For new customers who haven't purchased, we'll offer a discount in the next email to encourage them to take the final step. Additionally, we want to provide support by addressing any objections or unanswered questions that may have prevented them from completing their purchase.
